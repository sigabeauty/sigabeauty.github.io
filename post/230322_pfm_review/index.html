Pretrained Foundation Models

Mar 22, 2023
1 min read
research

ChatGPT shows great performance in few-shot and zero-shot conditions, it make researchers belive large pretrain model is the right path of AI future.

PFM (Pretrained Foundation Models), a huge data based model as a base of difference downstream tasks. Some PFM like BERT, GPT-3, MAE, DALLE-E and the recently hot ChatGPT are well know PFM, which give good initial paramters for different downstream tasks. The pretrain technique behind PFM play an important rool in application of large AI models, different from deep feature learning like CNN and RNN, GPT use transformer for deep feature learning, and use auto regressive training with large dataset.

Adventages of PFMs:

  1. Only need a few finetuning, we can get a great improvement on downstream tasks.
  2. PFMs have been tested as their performance.

In case to build a model to solve a target problem, it’ll be a good choice to use FPMs and finetune on the task related data.

Future, some works about how to make more efficient large model, security issue and model compression will appear.
